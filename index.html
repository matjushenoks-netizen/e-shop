About

Life is a moment — arriving from nowhere and disappearing into nowhere. The road we walk has no map, no clear destination, and none of us knows where death will wait for us.

When you remember this, everything in the world becomes small, fragile, almost weightless. And in that weightlessness comes clarity — a kind of awakening.

I capture these moments before they vanish: cities, oceans, horizons, and the silent truth behind them. In my videos and photographs, I try to preserve the only thing that truly exists — the present moment, before it slips away forever.
